About Doors

Discover Some of The Doors Special Features of Saudi Doors Factory

Impact Resistant Doors:

Our doors are resistant to shocks and breakage thanks to the use of high-quality materials and robust designs. They are designed to provide durable protection against damage and breakage, making them ideal for use in buildings, homes, and housing in general.

Moisture-Resistant Doors:

W.P.C doors are made from waterproof and moisture-resistant materials, making them ideal for use in areas prone to moisture and water exposure, such as bathrooms and kitchens.

Soundproof Doors:

Soundproof W.P.C doors are made from high-quality insulating materials, which help reduce noise transmission and provide a quiet and comfortable environment inside the home.

About Windows

Discover Some of The Window Special Features of Saudi Doors Factory

Heat-resistant Windows

U.P.V.C and aluminum windows offer superior protection and high security for the home, featuring durable and break-resistant designs. Additionally, these windows provide effective noise insulation to maintain protection and comfort within the home.

Custom-Designed Windows

U.P.V.C and aluminum windows feature unique designs that perfectly meet your needs, allowing you to choose the shape, size, and style that complements your home decor.

Soundproof Windows

U.P.V.C and aluminum windows are soundproof and thermally insulated, providing comfort and tranquility inside the home. Additionally, their durability and strength contribute to effective thermal and sound insulation within the residence.

About Us

Know More About Saudi Doors

Saudi Doors is a leading factory in Saudi Arabia specializing in manufacturing P.V.C and W.P.C. interior doors, as well as U.P.V.C and aluminum windows. The factory boasts a modern manufacturing unit equipped with state-of-the-art facilities and employs a team of highly skilled engineers and technicians. We offer a diverse range of doors and windows that combine elegance with high performance, always striving to achieve customer satisfaction through high-quality products and excellent service. Our network of showrooms across Saudi Arabia ensures easy access to our products in various major cities.

Customized and Personalized Design

Whether you prefer classic, traditional, or modern styles, you can choose from over 1000 specially designed designs to suit your taste, using high-quality materials such as PVC, UPVC, WPC, and aluminum. You can also request a custom design according to your preferences.

High-quality Manufacturing

We specialize in manufacturing high-quality doors and windows using advanced machinery such as thermal compression machines, drilling and pressing machines, heat welding machines, and electronic multi-head cutting machines.

Installation and After-Sales Service

We guarantee your comfort post-purchase through our exceptional after-sales service. This includes immediate support and specialized maintenance for a period of 10 years after the installation of doors and windows. Additionally, our branches are available in 13 locations across the kingdom to swiftly and efficiently meet your needs.
